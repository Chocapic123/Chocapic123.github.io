My favourite animal...

is Raven!

Ravens are really smart and beautiful animals.
For example they are very unforgiving and can even plan a revenge on somebody.
They have black feathers. The common raven averages 63 centimetres (25 inches) in length and 1.2 kilograms (2.6 pounds) in mass.
Although their typical lifespan is considerably shorter, common ravens can live more than 23 years in the wild.
